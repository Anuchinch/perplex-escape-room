    You look closer at the main portion of the clock. There is a numerical lock on the latch at the back. You try setting it to: Submit
    The Writing Desk

    Success
    The lock clicks open. A man squints out. You slowly coax him to leave the clock. He is the Head Scientist at BARC, and thanks you for your rescue.
